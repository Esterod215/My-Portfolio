Fairshare

This application is catered to people who share a living/work space. You can assign and keep track of tasks as well as view member contributions.

This project was part of Lambda School's 5 week Labs program. My team and I had to build upon and existing codebase. We added features that allowed for users to manage their tasks that has to be done for their shared living environment.

Front-End Tech: React,MDBReact,CSS,Auth0
Back-End Tech: Node, express, Auth0
